Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the membership? +
All memberships include access to our state-of-the-art equipment, locker rooms, and basic amenities. Premium and Elite members get additional benefits like group classes, pool access, and personal training discounts.
Can I cancel my membership? +
Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. Basic members can cancel with 30 days' notice, while Premium and Elite members have flexible cancellation options.
Do you offer student discounts? +
Yes, we offer a 15% discount for students with valid ID on all membership plans. Please bring your student ID when you sign up.
Are personal trainers available? +
Yes, we have certified personal trainers available. Elite members get unlimited sessions, while Premium members receive discounted rates. Basic members can purchase sessions separately.
What are the gym hours? +
Basic members: 5 AM - 11 PM daily. Premium and Elite members enjoy 24/7 access with secure keycard entry. Staffed hours are 5 AM - 11 PM daily.
